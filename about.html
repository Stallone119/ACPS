History & Foundation

Anointed Child Preparatory School was established in 2005 with a clear vision: to provide quality education that combines academic excellence with strong Christian values. Our founders recognized the need for an educational institution that would not only prepare children academically but also nurture their spiritual and moral development.

Starting with just 35 students in temporary facilities, the school has grown significantly over the years. Through dedication and commitment to our mission, we have expanded our infrastructure, developed comprehensive academic programs, and built a reputation for excellence in the Atiwa East District and beyond.

Today, we serve over 300 students from Nursery to Primary 6, with modern facilities, qualified teaching staff, and a curriculum that meets both national standards and international best practices.
