Message from the Headmaster

Dear Parents, Students, and Well-wishers,

It is with great pleasure and a deep sense of responsibility that I welcome you to Anointed Child Preparatory School. As the Headmaster, I am committed to upholding our school's vision of providing quality education rooted in Christian values and academic excellence.

Our mission extends beyond academic achievement to include character formation, spiritual growth, and the development of well-rounded individuals who will positively impact their communities. We believe that every child is uniquely gifted and deserves an environment that nurtures their God-given potential.

At ACPS, we pride ourselves on our dedicated teaching staff, comprehensive curriculum, and state-of-the-art facilities that provide our students with the tools they need to succeed. We emphasize critical thinking, creativity, and moral integrity in all aspects of our educational program.

I invite you to partner with us in this noble endeavor of shaping young minds and building future leaders. Together, we can provide our children with the foundation they need for lifelong success.

Yours in Service,
Mr. Emmanuel Ening William
Headmaster
