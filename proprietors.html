School Proprietors

Meet the visionary founders and proprietors whose dedication and commitment laid the foundation for our school's success.

Founding Visionaries

The proprietors of Anointed Child Preparatory School are dedicated individuals committed to educational excellence and community development.

Rev. Dr. Michael Asante

Founder & Chairman

Rev. Dr. Michael Asante is an ordained minister and educator with over 25 years of experience in both religious and educational leadership. His vision for establishing Anointed Child Preparatory School stemmed from his passion for combining quality education with strong Christian values.

With a Doctorate in Educational Leadership and a Master of Divinity, Rev. Dr. Asante has been instrumental in shaping the school's philosophy and ensuring its alignment with both educational best practices and Christian principles.

Qualifications

  • Ed.D. Educational Leadership
  • M.Div. Master of Divinity
  • B.A. Theology & Education

Mrs. Elizabeth Asante

Co-founder & Director

Mrs. Elizabeth Asante brings extensive experience in early childhood education and school administration. As co-founder, she has been pivotal in developing the school's curriculum and establishing its nurturing learning environment.

Her background in child psychology and educational management has contributed significantly to the school's holistic approach to education, ensuring that each child receives both academic instruction and emotional support.

Qualifications

  • M.Ed. Early Childhood Education
  • B.Sc. Psychology
  • Diploma in School Management

Elder Stephen Owusu

Board Member

Elder Stephen Owusu is a respected community leader and business professional who has supported the school's growth and development since its inception. His expertise in business management and community relations has been invaluable in establishing the school as a respected institution in the region.

As a board member, he provides strategic direction and ensures the school's financial sustainability while maintaining its educational mission.

Qualifications

  • MBA Business Administration
  • B.Sc. Economics
  • Certified Public Accountant

School Governance

The school is governed by a Board of Directors comprising the proprietors and selected education experts. The board provides strategic oversight, ensures financial accountability, and maintains the school's vision and mission.

Regular board meetings are held to review school performance, approve major decisions, and plan for future development. The board works closely with the Headmaster and teaching staff to ensure the highest standards of education are maintained.
