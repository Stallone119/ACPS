Headteacher's Welcome

Dear Parents, Guardians, and Students,

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to Anointed Child Preparatory School, where we are committed to providing quality education in a nurturing Christian environment. Yours in Service,

Academic Programs

At Anointed Child Preparatory School, we offer a comprehensive curriculum that balances academic excellence with character development. Our programs are designed to nurture curious minds and prepare students for future academic success.

Kindergarten

Our kindergarten program provides a nurturing environment where young children develop foundational skills through play-based learning. We focus on literacy, numeracy, social skills, and creative expression to prepare children for primary education.

Lower Primary

In lower primary (Classes 1-3), we build strong foundations in English, Mathematics, Science, and our local languages. Our holistic approach ensures children develop critical thinking skills while exploring their environment and community.

Upper Primary

Our upper primary program (Classes 4-6) challenges students with an expanded curriculum that includes Science, Mathematics, ICT, and French. We prepare students for leadership and responsibility.

Junior High School

Our Junior High School Program challenges students with an expanded curriculum that includes Social Studies, ICT, and French. We prepare students for the Basic Education Certificate Examination while fostering leadership and responsibility.

School Status

We offer both Day and Boarding options to meet the diverse needs of our students and their families. Each option provides a supportive environment for academic excellence and personal growth.

Day School

For students who prefer to return home after school hours

Features:

  • School hours: 7:30 AM - 3:30 PM
  • After-school care available until 5:30 PM
  • Daily homework supervision
  • Extracurricular activities
  • Regular parent-teacher interaction
  • Transportation services available

Boarding School

Full residential program with comprehensive care

Features:

  • 24/7 supervised accommodation
  • Structured study hours
  • Nutritious meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner)
  • Weekend activities and excursions
  • Laundry and housekeeping services
  • Medical care available
