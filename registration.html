Required Documents

Please have the following documents ready for submission after completing the online form:

  • Birth Certificate (Original and Photocopy)
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs
  • Previous school report (if applicable)
  • Immunization records
  • Parent/Guardian ID copy
  • Transfer certificate (for transfers from other schools)

Document Submission Options

In Person

Bring documents to the school office during working hours (7:30 AM - 4:30 PM, Monday-Friday)

Email

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the registration deadline?

Registration is open throughout the year, but we recommend applying at least one month before the term begins to secure a place.

Is there an application fee?

Yes, there is a non-refundable application fee of GH₵50. This can be paid at the school office or via mobile money.

When will I hear back after submitting the form?

Our admissions team will contact you within 3-5 working days to schedule an assessment and interview.

What if I need help filling out the form?

Our staff is available to assist you. Please visit the school office or call +233 24 123 4568 for assistance.
